About AituTech

In the vibrant city of Nur-Sultan, amidst the intellectual buzz of Aitu University, two driven young entrepreneurs, Yernur and Aidos, forged a partnership that would leave a lasting mark on the tech market. Fueled by their shared passion for technology and their alma mater, they laid the foundation for AituTech, a company dedicated to curating and selling the latest tech products. Yernur and Aidos, both alumni of Aitu University, recognized the need for a platform that could connect tech enthusiasts with cutting-edge products. Leveraging their intimate knowledge of the market and their deep understanding of consumer preferences, they meticulously handpicked a selection of top-tier tech gadgets and accessories, ranging from state-of-the-art smartphones to high-performance laptops and innovative smart home devices.

Aidos Tleukanov

Aidos, an alum of Aitu University, is a visionary with a deep passion for technology. His innovative mindset and commitment to excellence have played a pivotal role in shaping AituTech's success.

Yernur Kaisaruly

Yernur, also an Aitu University alum, brings extensive market knowledge and a keen eye for quality to AituTech. His dedication to customer satisfaction has been instrumental in establishing AituTech as a trusted name in the tech industry.

Our Vision

The success of AituTech not only cemented Yernur and Aidos' position as influential figures in the tech retail sector but also served as an inspiration to aspiring entrepreneurs within the Aitu University community and beyond. Their journey exemplified the transformative power of collaboration, determination, and a shared passion for technology, proving that with a strong foundation and a clear vision, even the most ambitious dreams can become a reality.
